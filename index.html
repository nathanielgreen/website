Ushare.

Ushare is an Android & iOS app that matches users based on their current geolocation and their destination, to find others around them whom they can share an Uber Cab with.

This functionality provides users with a way to split the fare of an Uber Cab with other users when their friends or colleagues may not be available.

The process of using Ushare is fast and simple, providing people a quick and easy way to reduce the cost of their travel.
